History

Our History

Omos Fashion School, founded in 2021, specializes in stylish women's wear, providing students with expert guidance in design, pattern-making, and sewing. The school offers high-quality materials, hands-on learning, and showcases for students to launch successful careers. Its mission is to nurture creativity and refine students' craft.

About Us

Meet Omos

Funmi Ikponmwosa, CEO and visionary behind Omos Fashion School, established the school with a passion for fashion and a mission to nurture aspiring designers. Her innovative leadership emphasizes hands-on learning and high-quality materials, guiding students to success. Under her mentorship, Omos Fashion School has become a respected institution for women's fashion design.
